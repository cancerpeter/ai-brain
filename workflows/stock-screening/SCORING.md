# Stock Screening Scoring

Total score: 100

## Trend: 30

- Price above `MA120`: 10
- Price above `MA60`: 8
- `MA20 > MA60`: 6
- 20-day breakout or near-high structure: 6

## Momentum: 20

- `RSI14` in a healthy range such as `50-70`: 8
- Positive `MACD` or improving `MACD`: 8
- Positive 20-day return: 4

## Relative Strength: 20

- 20-day return better than benchmark: 10
- 60-day return better than benchmark: 10

## Volume and Liquidity: 15

- Volume above 20-day average without extreme blow-off behavior: 6
- Adequate traded value for market participation: 5
- Turnover supportive but not overly speculative: 4

## Risk Control: 15

- 20-day volatility not extreme: 5
- 60-day max drawdown controlled: 5
- No obvious exhaustion pattern in the previous session: 5

## Suggested Interpretation

- `80-100`: strong candidate for watchlist and deeper research
- `65-79`: secondary candidate, worth tracking
- `50-64`: mixed profile, observe but do not prioritize
- `<50`: generally not suitable for medium- to long-term follow-up

## Hard Filters

Reject or heavily penalize stocks with:

- extremely weak liquidity
- clear long-term downtrend
- abnormal one-day speculative spikes without trend base
- excessive recent drawdown relative to peers
